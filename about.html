About Us - Gomato

Who We Are

Gomato is a food discovery platform designed to help people find amazing recipes with just a search. We use public APIs to deliver detailed cooking instructions, photos, ingredients, and dish origins.

Our Mission

We aim to inspire home chefs, students, and food lovers across India by making it easy to explore global cuisines and traditional Indian dishes.

Technologies Used

  • HTML, CSS & JavaScript
  • Live food database via TheMealDB API
  • Responsive design for mobile & desktop
